    About Jeremi Chiu

    My name is Jeremi Chiu. Another techie, and outdoor enthusiast from Vancouver, BC. I’m into bicycles, DIY Projects, motorcycles, and learning new languages (both programming and linguistic). At the moment I’m making a lot of use from Duolingo.

    When I’m not training for Gran Fondo’s, planning my next DIY project, or out for a ride, I work as a technical support analyst. On any given day I could be helping people debug their Wordpress, Dreamweaver, or hand coded HTML website, getting SSL certificates installed and validated, looking at and fixing DNS Zone Files, or updating/modifying a website based on mock-ups or examples supplied. Every day is a new challenge and I’ve become a bit of a jack of all trades.

    My educational background is in GIS (Geographic Information Systems) and my Alma Mater is the University of Lethbridge. I have a double major in Geography and Computer Science with a GIS Specialization. If Chicago didn’t already have the nickname “The Windy City”, Lethbridge would most definitely be a top contender.

    If that wasn’t enough to keep me busy I also have a love of food and cooking, and when I get the time, I pick a dish and gather the ingredients to make it. I have a love for desserts, and in another life I think I’d make a great pâtissier.

    About the Site

    This site was made using Jekyll along with Michael Roses’ Minimal Mistakes Jekyll theme. I decided to build a static site using Jekyll because it was more “bare metal” to develop compared to a dynamically generated site using a CMS. The security and performance improvements are extremely attractive too.
